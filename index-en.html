Our Story

The Spirit of Sainte Geneviève

Véronique and Marc welcome you to their 18th-century farm, nestled in a verdant setting in the heart of Lorraine. This historic place has been fully restored to offer all modern comforts while preserving the charm of yesteryear.

Ideally located between the cities of art and history of Metz and Nancy, our guest house is the perfect starting point to explore the cultural and natural riches of the region, from Place Stanislas to the Cathedral of Saint-Étienne.

We have been passionate about our beautiful English garden for many years, with its ancient roses, rare shrubs and panoramic views.
